Key Metrics Guide: P/E = Price / Earnings — lower is cheaper relative to profits. P/FCF = Price / Free Cash Flow — critical for capital-intensive oil firms; <15 is attractive. EV/EBITDA = Enterprise Value / EBITDA — sector standard; oil majors typically 5–10×. Div. Yield = Annual dividend as % of price — oil majors often 3–6%. ROE = Return on Equity — measures how efficiently capital is deployed.
